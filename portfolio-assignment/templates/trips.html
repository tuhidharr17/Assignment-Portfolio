My Trip to Margan House, Kalimpong

During my visit to Kalimpong, I had the pleasure of staying at the beautiful Margan House, nestled amidst the serene hills of the Himalayas. The journey to Margan House was filled with breathtaking views of lush greenery and picturesque landscapes.

The ambiance of Margan House was truly enchanting, offering a perfect blend of comfort and tranquility. From cozy rooms with stunning mountain views to delicious homemade meals served with warmth, every aspect of my stay was memorable.

One of the highlights of my trip was exploring the surrounding areas of Kalimpong, including visits to local markets, monasteries, and scenic viewpoints. The hospitality of the staff at Margan House and the beauty of Kalimpong left a lasting impression on me.

Overall, my experience at Margan House was nothing short of magical, and I look forward to returning to this hidden gem amidst the Himalayas.
